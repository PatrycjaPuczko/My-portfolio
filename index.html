I was a student of chemistry and environment protection at West Pomeranian University of Technology in Szczecin. I graduated with the Engineer’s and Master’s degrees.
It was a great experience. I learned a lot.
I was a member of two student organisations and the board president of Student Research Group.

For the last six years I was working as a specialist for environmental education and I was a project manager in League for the Preservation of Nature - the oldest non profit organisation connected with nature preservation and environment protection in Poland.

Since 2012 I have been a board member of West Pomeranian branch of the organisation. During my work I assisted in 5 big nationwide educational projects about biodiversity, biofuels, water preservation, species conservation and
I was a manager in several smaller ones.

At that time I was responsible for preparing all the project documentation, contacts with the subcontractors and experts, organisation of events (seminars, workshops, conferences), contact with media, promotion in social media and administration of the web pages.

I was the author of a few educational posters, brochures and articles. The preparation of conferences and promotional materials, posters, diplomas, certificates and logotypes gave me the knowledge about graphic software.

I really love cars, motorcycles, planes so from time to time I help my dad who is an automotive journalist mainly with interviews in English and I also take photos at automotive events (Geneva Motor Show, Berliner Motorrad Tage, ILA Berlin Air Show, Branna Okruh and a lot more. Moreover, I used to make clips and movies in Adobe Premiere Pro for some magazines.
